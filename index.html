Our Story
Born to Ride Fast
"No factory backing. No fancy sponsors. Just 7 riders, two tracks, and everything to prove."

The Nautanwa Speedemons are a street cycling crew built from the ground up — real riders, real streets, real competition. We started with nothing but bikes and the burning need to go faster than everyone else.

We train on the Tez Track (Tehsil Track) — a technical circuit packed with tight corners — and we have the High Straight for when it is time to find out who has the raw drag speed. No race victories yet, but that first win is coming.
